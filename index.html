Welcome
 

Hello, I am Anna Chashchyna, an ideologist, founder and consultant at Ecognize.me currently living and working in Kyiv, Ukraine. 

Ecognize.me is a boutique on environmental consultancy with the niche range of services concerned with Environment and Sustainable Development.

Ecognize.me is there to modulate human and industrial impact on environment by bridging the gap of innovation and societal change.

What we do

Life Cycle Assesment

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) is a tool for the systematic evaluation of the environmental aspects of a product or service system through all stages of its life cycle. LCA provides an adequate instrument for environmental decision support.

Sutainable Product Development

Includes the consideration of environmental, social, regulatory and economic factors for the development, production, use, and ultimate disposal of products.

Energy
Audit

Beyond identifying the sources of energy use, an energy audit seeks to prioritize the energy uses according to the greatest to least cost effective opportunities for energy savings.
